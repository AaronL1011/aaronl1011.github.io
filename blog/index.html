Smiths Lake

March 30, 2020

I have discovered one major benefit of our current pandemic. The fact that we now study remotely means I have the ability to go on tour with my coding! I have a couple close friends who live up in Smiths Lake and have been adament for me to come visit them for the past few months. Now that I don't have any commitements holding me in Sydney, I decided to pack up my laptop and go for a roadtrip (look I'm probably breaking some self-isolation rules but I'm fairly certain It will be fine).

So here we are, equipped with fast as F*CK internet, great company and lovely rain-forest esque scenery, it feels like I'm on vacation! Honestly if it wasn't so far from the majority of good tech jobs, I would probably be trying to move in here. And of course, the greatest perk of this whole trip, Pumpkin. Life is good.
